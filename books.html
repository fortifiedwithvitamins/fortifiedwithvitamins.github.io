Why I Write Book Notes

Recreational reading is something I enjoy, but also find challenging. I often struggle in following the author from page to page and remembering what a book is about after I finish it.

Derek Sivers and Nate Liason both write book reviews which can be found here and here. A book review requires reflection and acts as a resource for us to refer to in the future. This is a more active approach to reading, which Ali Abdaal discusses in this video.

These resources encouraged me to start writing my own book notes, which can be found below.
